C.       A brief on types of customers: 
- GSL caters to the individual /retail customers as well as the industrial customers. 
GSL business is divided into the following areas- 
  
 
a) The manufacturing business involves sale of ethanol, ENA and sale of by-
products. Ethanol is sold to OMC (HPCL, IOCL, and BPCL), ENA is sold to 
MNC’s like USL, Pernod, Beam, Bacardi and big domestic liquor companies 
like ABD, Radico and other country liquor bottlers. By products 
predominantly Animal Feed Supplement (AFS) is sold to local customers who 
lift the same. 
 
b) Consumer division involves sale of Country Liquor (Value segment), liquor in 
the value plus segment and IMFL (Indian Made Foreign Liquor). These are sold 
to the distributors, retailers etc. depending on the terms of trade and route to 
market.
