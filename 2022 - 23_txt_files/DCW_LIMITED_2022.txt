Describe the processes in place to safely reclaim your products 
for reusing, recycling and disposing at the end of life, for (a)

DCW Limited has a practice of packing the products in High

with a “Symbol or Reusing” to make the buyer aware of reusing 
our HDPE bags. The Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) of the
