our carbon footprint and contribute to a low-carbon

plastics and packaging, responsible sourcing and water 
management.
Our aim is to make a positive impact on the health of our 
Society, including our employees and the communities 
where we operate.

our business operations. We are committed to protecting 
human rights throughout our supply chain and are actively 
working to ensure that our suppliers adhere to ethical labour
