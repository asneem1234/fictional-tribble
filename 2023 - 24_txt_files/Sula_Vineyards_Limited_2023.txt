3.	
Describe the processes in place to safely reclaim your products for reusing, recycling and disposing at the end of life, for (a) 
Plastics (including packaging) (b) E-waste (c) Hazardous waste and (d) other waste.
	
The mechanism followed at Sula is:
 
 
Plastics (including Packaging): We intend to collect the plastic material for recycling, as per the Extended Producer 
Responsibility.
 
 
E-waste: All the E-Waste is sent to MSPCB authorized Recyclers only, for used batteries after their end of the life, we 
send to the vendor on buy back scheme.
 
 
Hazardous waste: We do not generate hazardous waste at our sites.
 
 
Other Waste: We have tie up with waste recyclers, who does the recycling by adding value to the waste.
