to data privacy. The company has adopted the Cyber Security Guidelines for Government Employees 
issued by the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology. Additionally, RailTel follows detailed 
directions under sub-section (6) of section 70B of the Information Technology Act 2000, which cover 
various aspects of information security practices, procedures, prevention, response, and reporting of 
cyber incidents. This framework includes features such as the collection, analysis, and dissemination 
of information on cyber incidents, forecasting and alerts for cyber security incidents, emergency 
measures for handling such incidents, coordination of response activities, and issuing guidelines,
