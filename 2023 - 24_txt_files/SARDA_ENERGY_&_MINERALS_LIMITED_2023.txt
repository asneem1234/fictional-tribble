We have long recognized the importance of Environment, Social 
and Governance (ESG). We have worked upon bringing positive 
change, not just within the industry but also in the communities

in pollution to meet the evolving stakeholder expectations. We 
continue to seek innovative ways for this transition, through 
experimentation and investment. While the Company explores 
sustainable alternatives to traditional steel production methods,

methods. We also ensure to safeguard the flora and fauna in the 
course of our operations.

business ethics, ever-evolving compliance landscape, and the
