The Company’s comprehensive sustainability strategy seamlessly incorporates ESG-related challenges into its decision making processes. On the environmental

all environmental rules and regulations in the locations where it operates. A robust internal mechanism is in  place to consistently review and evaluate the systems and 
processes for ensuring compliance.
