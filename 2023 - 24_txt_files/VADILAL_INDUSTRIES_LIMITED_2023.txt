process owners are responsible to ensure adequate controls are identiﬁed and implemented to control the identiﬁed risks.

actions. This is enabled through an online safety incident reporting tool which is accessible to all employees to facilitate 
transparent reporting. The platform also supports incident investigation and corrective action with the perspective of 
eliminating hazards and preventing incidents. The awareness to employees on incident reporting is created during through 
trainings and communication through E-mails .The action owners are also sensitized on the importance of taking corrective 
action within given timelines with the perspective of eliminating hazards and ensuring mitigation plan is implemented and 
employee can also report their health and safety related issues or concerns through an internal administration helpdesk and 
these issues are resolved by the concerned action owner within prescribed timelines. They can also email their concerns to 
the Corporate oﬃce or email ID and communicate with local health and safety teams.
